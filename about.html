About PAge

Home / About /

NAVAM”- An authentic mixture of Purity, Rarity and Quality…. In rising consumption of edible oil and sinking in quality of production, we “NAVAM” has come forward to uphold the integrity and commitment in extracting 100% pure edible and essential oils through effective measures of cold pressed and wood pressed process in the name Navam's "RAW & PURE".

“Navam” comprises in production of 9 different types of edible oil – All the raw products are grown naturally and organically without chemical or synthetic fertilizers are collected directly from farmers in order to recognize their commitment and contribution towards the agriculture. We want to bring back the hope and respect among the farmers in being addressing the nature.

In a vision to merit and endorse the primitive traditional method of oil extraction, “NAVAM” has adopted “Bull Driven Oil Ghana” and extraction of oil is completely from this process. This wood pressed method preserves all the flavor, natural physiological and chemical properties and most importantly its original taste. It’s our earnest endeavor to ensure that our consumers get hygienic, nutrient rich and best in class edible essential oil.
